OUR FACILITIES

Training Area

The training area is the core of the gym, where members can work on developing their strength and physical conditioning. This area is designed to offer a variety of equipment and tools that allow users to perform a wide range of exercises to strengthen and tone their muscles.

Equipment

Dumbbells: Dumbbells are versatile tools that allow for a greater variety of movements and exercises. They can be used to work individual muscle groups or for full-body exercises. Free Weights: Free weights, such as kettlebells and Olympic bars, provide an effective way to improve strength, balance, and coordination.

Stretching and Flexibility Area

Equipment designed to assist in stretching exercises, such as resistance bands, yoga blocks. These tools are helpful in increasing the effectiveness of stretches and improving flexibility.

Relaxation and Wellness Area

A comfortable area with seating where members can sit and relax after their workout sessions. Where you can chat with Joel about the workouts performed.

Cardio Zone

Cardiovascular equipment provides an effective way to improve cardiovascular endurance, burn calories, and strengthen the heart.

Changing Rooms and Showers

We provide users with a private and comfortable space to change, shower, and maintain their personal hygiene before and after their workouts.
